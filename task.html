Stages and Timelines

Level 1

Poster Submission Round (via Uptoskills)

1 2
Start: 15 Sep 24, 10:00 AM ISTEnd: 25 Aug 24, 11:59 PM IST

Each team is required to choose one business value and select a company/ organization that has built a viable business upholding the value as their core business philosophy. A poster shall be submitted demonstrating the importance/ impact of value-driven business. Teams may choose from a) Ethical Business, b) Inclusive Workplace, c) Employee Satisfaction, d) Social Impact, e) Environmental Sustainability. The list is not exhaustive. Teams may come up with different values and ideas. Only one poster should be submitted by the team. An executive summary of not more than 300 words should be submitted along with the poster. The poster and executive summary should be submitted in one PDF file ONLY The names of the participating team members should be included in the poster. Original images and data are preferred, if used secondary information is used, accurate citation is mandatory.

Level 2

Offline Case Study and Presentation Round

1 2
Start: 15 Sep 24, 10:00 AM ISTEnd: 25 Aug 24, 11:59 PM IST

Six shortlisted teams from the online submissions would be invited for a case study presentation at IRMA. On-Site Case Presentation would be on a case based on Ethics and Business Values.

Details:

All that you need to know about Case Study Competition on Business on Values We are delighted to invite all graduate students in management and related disciplines to join the first Case Study Competition on "Business on Values," hosted by the ESAF Chair for Business on Values at IRMA. Founded in 1979 by Dr. Verghese Kurien, IRMA has a rich legacy in rural management education and research. IRMA's mission is to empower rural communities and promote sustainable development. This competition aims to promote value-driven businesses emphasizing sustainability, ethics, and long-term value creation among budding managers and professionals Join us in this exciting competition to explore and promote value-driven businesses

Result of Round 1 31 Aug 24, 05:00 PM IST
Case Reveal for final Round 13 Sep 24, 05:00 PM IST
